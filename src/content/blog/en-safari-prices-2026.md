The cost of a Tanzania safari varies enormously depending on duration, accommodation type, season and which parks you visit. This guide breaks down real prices so you can plan your budget without surprises.

## Price Ranges by Category

### Budget Safari
**$150 — $250 USD per person/day**
Basic camping with tents, simple meals, shared vehicle with other travelers and a guide.

### Mid-range Safari
**$300 — $500 USD per person/day**
Lodges or permanent camps with private rooms, en-suite bathroom, full meals, private vehicle and dedicated guide. Best value for money.

### Luxury Safari
**$600 — $1,500+ USD per person/day**
Exclusive lodges or luxury mobile camps with suite tents, butler service, gourmet meals, premium private vehicle and expert guide.

## Package Examples

- **3 days — Tarangire + Ngorongoro**: from $900 (budget) to $3,000 (luxury) per person
- **5 days — Serengeti + Ngorongoro + Tarangire**: from $1,800 to $5,500 per person
- **7 days — Complete Northern Circuit**: from $2,800 to $9,000 per person
- **10 days — Safari + Zanzibar**: from $3,500 to $12,000+ per person

## Recommended Tips
- **Guide/driver**: $15-25 USD per person/day
- **Camp cook**: $5-10 USD per person/day
- **Lodge staff**: $10-20 USD total per stay
